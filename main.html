MADAN MAHAL FORT JABALPUR , MP

Mehrangarh Fort, also known as Madal Mahal, is a 15th-century fort in Jodhpur, India. Perched on a hill, it offers stunning views of the city. The fort features intricate architecture, palaces, temples, and a museum showcasing royal artifacts. Its rich history and grandeur make it a popular tourist destination.

DHUANDHAR WATERFALLS JABALPUR

The Dhuandhar Falls is a stunning waterfall located in Bhedaghat, near Jabalpur in Madhya Pradesh. The name “Dhuandhar” comes from the Hindi words “Dhuan” (smoke) and “Dhar” (flow), referring to the misty appearance created by the powerful plunge of the Narmada River1. The falls are about 30 meters high and are known for their roaring sound, which can be heard from a distance

CHAUSATH YOGINI TEMPLE IN JABALPUR

The Chausath Yogini Temple in Jabalpur, India, is a historic Hindu temple dedicated to the 64 yoginis (female mystics). Built in the 10th century, it features a unique circular structure with 64 shrines, each housing a yogini image. The temple showcases remarkable architecture and spiritual significance, attracting devotees and tourists alike.

HOTEL VIJAN MAHAL, JABALPUR

Hotel Vijan Mahal is a luxurious haven in the bustling city of Jabalpur, offering an unforgettable blend of warmth, comfort, and style. Nestled in a prime location, this 3-star hotel is a stone's throw away from the Jabalpur Junction Railway Station and within easy reach of the city's main attractions.

DUMNA PARK, JABALPUR

Dumna Park, Jabalpur: A serene eco-tourism destination with a lake, wildlife sanctuary, and birdwatching haven. Enjoy boating, trekking, jungle safaris, and picnic areas amidst lush greenery. Home to deer, sloth bears, monkeys, and 150+ bird species. Perfect for nature lovers and families. Entry fee: ₹20, Timings: 7 am - 7 pm.
